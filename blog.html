Diplomatic relations between Canada and the Philippines have been going on since 1949 after the end of World War 2. Cooperation between both countries are close, strong and friendly to promote democratic development, peace, and to protect human rights. Aside from the waste dispute that occurred back in May, Canada's reputation in the Philippines has been relatively high; Canadian products and services were known to be of high quality in the country.
