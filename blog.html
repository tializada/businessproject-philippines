Brands

Jolibee is a Filipino multinational fast food chain. There are currently around 1,000 stores open in the Philippines. In recent years, they have began expanding into Canadian markets. They are known for their fried chicken and rice, as well as their sweet spaghetti, which is popular among most Filipinos. Representing the fast food chain is a large, red, white, and yellow antropomorphic bee with a chef's hat.

Inventions


Dr. Fe del Mundo

Invented the bamboo incubator in 1941, which was to be easy to reproduce and more accessible to those who lived in rural communities or in areas with no electricity. She became the first woman to become a National Scientist in the Philippines in 1980.

Dr. Abelardo Aguilar

Discovered a new form of bacteria in his backyard in 1949. This led to the discovery of Erythromycin, an antibiotic used to treat respiratiory infections, Legionnaires disease, diphtheria, UTIs, and STIs such as syphilis.
